Find Your Ring Size

A photo won't be perfectly precise on its own \u2014 that's why we ask for 3 photos, not one, and compare them.
  1. Place a real coin flat on a table, right next to your finger \u2014 same surface, same distance from the camera
  2. Relax your hand, don't spread your finger wide
  3. Take the photo straight down, not at an angle
  4. Repeat 3 times \u2014 slightly different position each time is fine, that's the point
Not confident in this result? A photo estimate is a convenience, not a guarantee. Measure with a string or strip of paper around your finger and compare to a printed ring sizing chart, or visit any local jeweler \u2014 they'll size you for free in under a minute.
