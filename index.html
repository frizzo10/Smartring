P wave — atria contract
The small bump. Your atria squeeze blood into the ventricles, loading them for the main pump.
QRS spike — ventricles fire
The tall spike. Both ventricles contract together, pushing blood to your lungs and body.
T wave — heart resets
The rounded hill. The muscle relaxes and recharges electrically for the next beat.
